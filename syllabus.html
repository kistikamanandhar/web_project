>What is BIM?

Bachelor of Information Management (BIM) is a four-year program offered by Tribhuvan University (TU), designed to provide comprehensive knowledge in Management Information Systems and Communication Systems. This program equips students with essential software development skills, particularly in object-oriented software design methods and data management systems.
